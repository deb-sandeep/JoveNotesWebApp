  • JoveNotes
    • Concept
      • Driver
        • Effectivize and accelerate study, learning and education process
      • How?
        • Minimize study time
        • Increase efficiency of learning
          • Lessen time spent on retention & recollection
            • Without sacrificing depth or breadth of coverage
          • Increase time availability on problem solving (applicability)
          • Gamify the learning experience
          • Adaptive zero down on only the areas which need attention
        • Increase efficiency of teaching
          • Clear distinction between operational, support and coaching mode of teaching
          • Limited intervention in imposing 'the right way'
          • Precise triggers for coaching
        • Introduce computer assisted
          • Data capture
          • Analytics driven feedback
          • AI driven recommendation engine
          • Adaptive body of knowledge
        • Creating a stress free study environment
        • Shift focus from teaching to coaching
        • Keep learning contextual and synergized with school
      • Solution
        • A computing system which
          • Operates on digitized knowledge base

          • Is able to increase triangulation of knowledge points

            by deriving questions on its own

          • Adapts to students style of study
          • Handles the tracking, presentment and feedback on study progress
          • Gamifies the study process
          • Presents interactive questions
          • Uses contextual multimedia and study assits
          • Builds best practices of problem solving
        • Changes existing paradigms
          • A mentor need not be more knowledgeable than the student
          • Driving learning my making schedules is grossly inefficient
          • We should break away from siloed mode of education
          • We should be able to tie all the subjects together
          • There is no lower time bound for effective study
          • Study and learning should not be environment dependent
